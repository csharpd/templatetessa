Fees

My fee per session is £60, payable monthly in advance. Following an initial interview, I ask new patients to commit to a trial period of 6 weeks. I hold open two reduced price places for those on low incomes.

Sessions are typically once a week, with a commitment from both of us to an initial six-week period. The session time is 60 minutes. In times of crisis, sessions can be increased to twice weekly by mutual agreement
