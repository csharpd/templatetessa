Fees

It’s important that you find a psychotherapist you feel comfortable about working with. Once we’ve made contact, the next step is to set up an initial interview in person. If both of us feel it’s a good fit, we commit to a trial period of 6 weeks.

My fee per session is £60, payable monthly in advance. I hold open two reduced price places for those on low incomes. Sessions are typically once a week, with a commitment from both of us to an initial six-week period. The session time is 60 minutes. In times of crisis, sessions can be increased to twice weekly by mutual agreement
